The dataset

The data in this dashboard is based on the list of grants to associations approved or not by the City of Paris since 2013 (and by the Department of Paris from 2013 to 2018, when it merged with the City of Paris).

Analytical Dashboard

An analytical dashboard is a type of dashboard composed of a large amount of data, created and used by analysts to provide support to management. It provides companies with a complete data overview, middle management plays a crucial role in its use.

The purpose of this dashboard is to identify trends that can help make decisions for the future by allowing people to interact, learn and explore data from the past few years.

Strategic Dashboard

A strategic dashboard helps to steer strategic choices and to monitor the actions undertaken. It facilitates the overall company management and its activities, while respecting the major balances. It enables the achievement of strategic objectives to be monitored.

This dashboard aims to summarize performance through KPIs over the past year (2021) in order to monitor Paris city long term strategy.

Which associations ?

Associations governed by the Law of July 1, 1901 that have submitted one or more grant applications to the City of Paris (and the Department of Paris from 2013 to 2018, when it merged with the City of Paris) are considered grant recipients.

Why Metabase ?

It is a lightweight open-source tool. It is very easy to use with a clean and dynamic user interface. A large number and type of charts are available and its deployment is supported by Heroku.
